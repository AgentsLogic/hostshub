TWIN HILLS RIVER RANCH

GOLDTHWAITE, TX

A Premier Texas Hill Country Destination

Twin Hills River Ranch offers a truly exceptional experience in the heart of Texas Hill Country. This luxury estate spans nearly 80 acres of pristine land overlooking the picturesque Colorado River, providing the perfect backdrop for unforgettable getaways.

Property Highlights

  • Luxury Accommodations: Over 5,120 sq. ft. of luxurious living space
  • Sleeping Capacity: 6 bedrooms that can accommodate 20+ guests
  • River Access: Private access to the Colorado River for fishing and water activities
  • Outdoor Activities: Dedicated gun range, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing
  • Amenities: Fully equipped gourmet kitchen, spacious living areas, outdoor entertainment spaces
  • Privacy: Secluded location offering tranquility and privacy for your group

The Perfect Retreat for Groups

Whether you're planning a family reunion, corporate retreat, or a gathering of friends, Twin Hills River Ranch provides the space and amenities for an unforgettable experience. The property's expansive grounds offer numerous opportunities for outdoor recreation, while the luxurious home provides comfortable accommodations for everyone in your group.

The ranch's strategic location offers the perfect balance of seclusion and accessibility. You'll enjoy the privacy of a remote getaway while still being within reach of local attractions and conveniences.
